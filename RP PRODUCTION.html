About Us

Crafting Stories Through Cinema

10+ Years of Excellence

RP PRODUCTION is a premier media production house based in India, specializing in wedding cinematography, event coverage, live streaming, and commercial filmmaking. We blend technical mastery with artistic vision to deliver visuals that resonate.

Our mission is to elevate every frame — from intimate pre-wedding shoots to grand destination weddings, corporate galas, and music videos. We don't just document; we direct, design, and deliver cinematic experiences.

Why choose us? Because we treat every project like a feature film. With state-of-the-art RED cameras, DJI drones, Jimmy Jib cranes, and a team of award-winning cinematographers, we bring Hollywood-grade production to your story.
